Training Pipeline Roadmap

  • 1. Frame Extraction

    Cameras record at 30Hz, while sensors record at 50Hz. Extract precise frames tied to UNIX timestamps.

  • 2. Auto-Annotation (CLIP)

    Automated HuggingFace Zero-Shot tagging across the frame directory.

  • 3. Manual Feature Refinement

    Review frames and manually draw missed bounding boxes through interactive Video-Canvas alignment.

  • 4. High-Frequency Temporal Sync

    Weave video ground truth against sliding windows of purely 1-D physics telemetry streams.

  • 5. Core Physics Transformer Model

    PyTorch training utilizing high-batch sizes and validation slicing to yield absolute road classifications.

Next-Generation Capabilities

GPS Velocity Fusion

Mitigates static oscillation errors by mapping real-time speed over IMU coordinates. Ensures the system easily distinguishes driving fast on dirt from driving slowly on cobblestone.

Anomaly Gate (OOD)

Actively drops frames lacking rotational biking kinematics. If a rider steps off the bike and walks, the Out-of-Distribution safety net immediately isolates the signal logic to prevent false positives.

Active Learning Routing

Accelerate model refinement. Discover false predictions during inference and click a button to immediately package the 50Hz physics array & camera frame back into your local Annotation staging queue.

WebGL Digital Twin

Watch the actual structural vibrations map into a 3D simulation powered by Three.js. Visually diagnose pitch, yaw, and rapid Z-axis jolts during playback.

Diagnostic Analytics

Interactive cross-session Matrix and drift diagnostics directly within the UI. Generate rigorous, one-click PDF validation reports of the model's accuracy.

Video/Sensor Scrubber

Scrub back and forth through hours of video utilizing a tightly coupled time domain synchronization engine—instantly updating Maps, FFT Spectrograms, and 3D scenes.

Dual-Stream Fusion

Split your inputs so the model evaluates speed/GPS independently from the raw IMU shudder, then merges their insights.

Physics-Based Filtering

Use traditional DSP (Digital Signal Processing) to remove the "bicycle's signature" (tire bounce rate) from the IMU data so only the "road's signature" remains.

State-of-the-Art (SOTA) Analysis

A peer-reviewed architectural derivation highlighting critical divergences between the CycleSafe Multimodal framework and contemporary baseline methodologies within micro-mobility surface classification literature.
